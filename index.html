About Us


Our Background

We are a company who are well-versed in the fun and rigors of travelling with student groups. C and C Tours came together to help teachers plan and book school trips to Washington D.C. The company has been taking student groups to Washington D.C. for over 30 years! In recent years we have expanded to day trips and other destinations.

Our Services

Whether you're looking for a weekend trip to Washington or a day trip, we have you covered. Simply tell one of our specialists where you want to go, and we'll help you figure out the rest. We work directly insiders in the travel field to provide the most affordable student trips in the region.

Satisfaction Guaranteed

While working with us, we want you to be completely happy with the experience. If you have questions about us, our services, get in touch! We hope you continue to book with us for many years to come.
